About Us

This is a page showing information about myself. I will put my email contact information, and a short description of who I are. I'm a computer science student and Walla Walla University. I have been in love with computers ever since I was a child. I started off with lego robotics in school and enjoyed the challenge of creating instructions for the robot the most. So, when I discovered programming in high school I immediately fell in love with it. I did some basic classes over the summer and decided that I wanted to learn more. I went to Walla Walla University and enrolled in their computer science program. You can look at my github webpage to see some of the projects I have worked on. When you look at my repositories make sure you check for repositories with published github pages. I was good enough in my math and computer science classes that I got hired as a tutor and have been helping other students overcome their challenges and learn important computer science problem solving skills. I also got hired as a TA by one computer science teachers. He also hired me to help him with his personal project of a Python interpreter in Smalltalk. If you would like to see this project please contact me with the email below as it is a private repo.

Now, a little bit about why this project was important to me. I am a computer science major and love gaming. That's why I picked a project relating to Steam, the for most gaming vendor and platform on desktop. Since their user interface can be a bit unintuitive I wanted to make something that could help me see in just a few moments what games are on sale Hopefully this website will be able to help some of you out there in your efforts to have a good time and maybe save you some money in the process. This was originally a team effort with two of my colleagues but I decided to strip out my work on the project so I could have a better demonstration of my skills. I also launched this on github pages so it is easier to show to prospective employers.
