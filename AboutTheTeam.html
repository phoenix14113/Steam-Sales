If you would like to see this project please contact me with the email below, as it is a private repo.

I am a computer science major and love gaming. I picked a project relating to Steam because it is the foremost gaming vendor and platform on desktop. Their user interface can be a bit unintuitive. To help with that, I wanted to make a program that could help their inefficiency. This program helps you see, in just a few moments, what games your friends have that are on sale.
